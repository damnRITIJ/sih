# Suicidal Thoughts (⚠️ High Risk)

## Causes
- Severe depression
- Hopelessness
- Trauma
- Chronic stress

## Symptoms
- Talking about death
- Withdrawing from others
- Giving away possessions

## Coping Strategies
- Reach out to a trusted friend or family member immediately  
- Contact a counselor or helpline  
- Avoid isolation  

## When to Seek Help
**Always** – treat as an emergency.
